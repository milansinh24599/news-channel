The number of people infected by the coronavirus


in India crossed 1 million, nearly five-and-a half months after the country reported its first case. The US and Brazil are the only countries that have registered cases in excess of this figure, although the pace of spread there has been much faster.

The pandemic has so far claimed more than 25,000 lives in India. Relative to population, India’s numbers are still low, but the steep rise in absolute numbers risks overwhelming the healthcare system, apart from triggering more restrictions that are already hampering economic recovery.
